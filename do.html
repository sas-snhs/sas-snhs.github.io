EAA Science Buddies

Tuesdays 3:15 - 4:15 Room H-103(Dr.Belcher's room) From powering batteries to learning about density through DIY lava lamps and even exploring the SAS rainforest, EAA Science Buddies is an elementary after-school program designed to help younger students get hands-on experience with experiments spanning topics of chemistry, physics, and biology. This course is supervised by our high school physics teacher, Dr. Belcher and taught by SNHS members.

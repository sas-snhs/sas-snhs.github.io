Applications are sent by email around mid-December to sophomores, juniors, and seniors.

Requirements

Sophomores must be enrolled in/have completed at least one honors-level science course with an A average or higher, or must have obtained an A+ in any non-honors science courses.

Juniors and Seniors must have completed at least one honors-level science course with an A average or higher, have maintained an A average or better in any non-honors science courses, and maintain an average of A or higher in advanced science courses.

All SNHS members must be enrolled in an advanced science course during their junior year and must complete at least two advanced science courses across their junior and senior years.

The applicant's overall cumulative GPA must be 3.7 or higher.

Applicants deemed ineligible by any of the criteria above have an opportunity to apply and explain why they should be considered.

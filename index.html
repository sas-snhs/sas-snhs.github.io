Welcome to the official website for the Singaporean Amercian School chapter of the Science National Honor Society.

Our mission is to engender a new group of young thinkers who will be the future of industry, research, and scientific exploration. SNHS connects students with active and deep interests in science. Our involvement at SAS bring about an active pursuit of scientific knowledge not only for our members, but for the SAS community as a whole.
